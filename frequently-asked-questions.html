Frequently Asked Questions

Why to join NCC?

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement. It has enormous potential for nation building. The NCC provides opportunities to the youth of the country for their all-round development with a sense of Duty, Commitment, Dedication, Discipline and Moral Values so that they become able leaders and useful citizens. The NCC provides exposure to the cadets in a wide range of activities., with a distinct emphasis on Social Services, Discipline and Adventure Training. The NCC is open to all regular students of schools and colleges on a voluntary basis. The students have no liability for active military service.

What is the selection process to join ncc nitc ?

Students will be informed through Gmail at the time of enrollment. Then they can undergo a selection process after submitting the google form shared in mail. There are multiple levels in the selection process.

Who is the person incharge of ncc nitc?

ANO : ASSOCIATE NCC OFFICER is the person incharge.

Who can join NCC NITC ?

Any student who belongs to NITC can join NCC NITC.

Will joining NCC affect academics ?

No , it won’t affect. NCC NITC also prioritizes academics and supports students as much as possible.

What sort of activities are done in NCC NITC?

Camp training, Social service and community development, Sports, Adventure and basic learning, Personality development are the activities done in NCC NITC.

NCC NITC

NCC NITC , 9(K) Naval Unit NCC Kozhikode , is an effective organization built to mould the young minds of NITC into well disciplined and responsible citizens of India . It aims to spread the idea of selfless service and encourages to develop and enhance one's personality and other essential skills.
