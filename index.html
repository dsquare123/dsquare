Dr Prasad Krishna is an indelible figure in the history of National Institute of Technology, Calicut . His research, teaching and work experiences makes him the most suitable person as director of NITC.He was the former Head of the Mechanical Engineering Department of NIT, Surathkal and became the director of NITC in 2021.

Dr Prasad Krishna Director NIT Calicut

Dr. P S Sathidevi is the Deputy Director and Professor in Electronics department of National Institute of Technology, Calicut. Being an allumini, she adorn that privilege very well. And she also managed the institution as the head before the Director took charge.

Dr.P S Sathidevi Deputy Director NIT Calicut

Commander (Dr) Shamasundara M S was a Naval Officer prior to joining the Institute as Registrar. He was commissioned in the year 1999 in Engineering branch of the Navy. He had held various key operational, technical and staff appointments during his 23 years of service in the Indian Navy.

AIM OF NCC

The ‘Aims’ of the NCC laid out in 1988 have stood the test of time and continue to meet the requirements expected of it in the current socio–economic scenario of the country. The NCC aims at developing character, comradeship, discipline, a secular outlook, the spirit.

PLEDGE

We the cadets of the National Cadet Corps,
do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizens of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings.

ANO NCC NITC

NCC NITC

NCC NITC , 9(K) Naval Unit NCC Kozhikode , is an effective organization built to mould the young minds of NITC into well disciplined and responsible citizens of India . It aims to spread the idea of selfless service and encourages to develop and enhance one's personality and other essential skills.
